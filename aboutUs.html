Welcome to Akiba Savings, where we aim to provide users with a user-friendly interface to monitor and manage their finances effectively. Our mission is to empower individuals to make informed financial decisions and achieve their financial goals.

Foundation

Akiba Savings was founded with the goal of addressing the challenge of pooling all savings into a single account. We understand that managing finances can be overwhelming, and traditional banking systems may not always meet the needs of individuals seeking to allocate their funds into designated goals.

Our platform offers a solution by allowing users to create, track, and manage savings goals conveniently. Whether you're saving for a vacation, a new car, or a down payment on a house, Akiba Savings provides the tools you need to stay on track.

MVP Features

  • Enable users to create, edit, and delete savings goals.
  • Allow users to set a target amount and optionally, allocate funds towards each goal.
  • Allow users to view a list of their recent transactions.
  • Enable users to categorize transactions (e.g., income, expenses) and add notes if necessary.

Sponsors

Our sponsors are involved in a range of initiatives designed to benefit the community and promote positive change. Here are some ongoing initiatives supported by our sponsorship partners:

  • Financial Literacy Workshops: Our sponsors host workshops to educate people on financial planning, budgeting, and investment strategies.
  • Sports Events and Competitions: Several sponsors support sports events, including marathons, football tournaments, and basketball leagues, promoting health and fitness.
  • Art and Culture Festivals: We have sponsors who support local art and culture events, fostering creativity and providing platforms for artists to showcase their talents.
  • Environmental Conservation Programs: Some sponsors are actively involved in environmental conservation, supporting tree planting initiatives and beach clean-up events.
  • Educational Scholarships and Grants: Many sponsors contribute to educational scholarships and grants to support students in achieving their academic goals.
